How Notarization Works
  1. Your document is hashed locally (SHA-256) - the file never leaves your device
  2. The hash is sent to the smart contract on the blockchain
  3. MetaMask requests your signature to confirm the transaction
  4. Once confirmed, the hash is permanently stored with a timestamp
  5. You can verify the document anytime using the verification page
